# Research Scope

This project tracks the progress of AI labor replacement through public evidence.

Included:
- capability progress that affects real work
- enterprise adoption and workflow integration
- layoffs, hiring freezes, and substitution signals tied to AI
- national policy and institutional support or friction
- milestone events with explicit evidence

Excluded:
- hype-only social posts
- unattributed claims
- broad macro layoffs with no AI link
- opaque proprietary scoring with no source trace
